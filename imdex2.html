1940s

1943-1945

Captain America: The First Avenger

Steve Rogers transforms into Captain America and battles Red Skull and Hydra. First appearance of the Tesseract (Space Stone) and the foundation of S.H.I.E.L.D.

1990s

1995

Captain Marvel

Carol Danvers becomes Captain Marvel and helps the Skrulls find a new home. Nick Fury loses his eye and starts the Avengers Initiative.

2008

2008

Iron Man

Tony Stark builds his first Iron Man suit and becomes a superhero. The beginning of the MCU and Tony's journey as Iron Man.

2008

The Incredible Hulk

Bruce Banner struggles with his transformation into the Hulk while being hunted by General Ross and facing the Abomination.

2010

2010

Iron Man 2

Tony faces Ivan Vanko and government pressure. Introduction of Black Widow and War Machine. Nick Fury approaches Tony about the Avengers.

2012

2012

The Avengers

Earth's Mightiest Heroes assemble for the first time to stop Loki and the Chitauri invasion of New York. The Mind Stone is introduced.

2013

2013

Iron Man 3

Tony deals with PTSD after the Battle of New York while facing the Mandarin and Aldrich Killian. Tony destroys all his suits.

2014

2014

Thor: The Dark World

Thor battles Malekith and the Dark Elves. The Reality Stone (Aether) is introduced. Loki fakes his death and impersonates Odin.

2014

Captain America: The Winter Soldier

Steve discovers Hydra has infiltrated S.H.I.E.L.D. and faces his old friend Bucky Barnes as the Winter Soldier. S.H.I.E.L.D. falls.

2014

Guardians of the Galaxy

Peter Quill forms the Guardians and battles Ronan. The Power Stone is introduced. First mention of Thanos as a major threat.

2014

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2

Peter meets his father Ego while the Guardians face internal conflicts. The team grows stronger as a family unit.

2015

2015

Avengers: Age of Ultron

Tony creates Ultron who becomes a global threat. Introduction of Vision, Scarlet Witch, and Quicksilver. The Mind Stone powers Vision.

2015

Ant-Man

Scott Lang becomes the new Ant-Man with help from Hank Pym. They stop Darren Cross and his Yellowjacket suit from being weaponized.

2016

2016

Captain America: Civil War

The Avengers split over the Sokovia Accords. Zemo reveals the truth about Tony's parents. Introduction of Black Panther and Spider-Man.

2016

Black Widow

Natasha confronts her past and reunites with her Russian spy "family" to take down the Red Room and free the Widows.

2016

Spider-Man: Homecoming

Peter Parker balances high school life with being Spider-Man while stopping Vulture's illegal arms dealing operation.

2017

2017

Doctor Strange

Stephen Strange becomes the Sorcerer Supreme and protects Earth from mystical threats. Introduction of the Time Stone and the multiverse concept.

2017

Black Panther

T'Challa becomes king of Wakanda and faces Killmonger's challenge. Wakanda opens up to the world, revealing their advanced technology.

2017

Thor: Ragnarok

Thor discovers his sister Hela and witnesses the destruction of Asgard. Thor reunites with Hulk and leads the Asgardian people to safety.

2018

2018

Ant-Man and the Wasp

Scott Lang partners with Hope van Dyne to rescue Janet from the Quantum Realm while evading both the law and a mysterious new enemy.

2018

Avengers: Infinity War

Thanos collects the Infinity Stones and succeeds in snapping away half of all life in the universe. The Avengers' greatest defeat.

2023

2019/2023

Avengers: Endgame

The remaining Avengers execute a time heist to collect the Infinity Stones and reverse the Snap. Tony Stark's ultimate sacrifice saves the universe.
